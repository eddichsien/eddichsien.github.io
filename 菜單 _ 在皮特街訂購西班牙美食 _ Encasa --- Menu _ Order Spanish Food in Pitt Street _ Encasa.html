Cava

Cava is a term used to describe a specific type of sparkling wine that originates from Catalonya, Spain. It is often compared to Champagne in terms of production method and style, though there are some differences. Cava is made using the traditional method, also known as the "Champagne method" or "methode traditionnelle," where the secondary fermentation, which creates the bubbles, occurs inside the bottle.
